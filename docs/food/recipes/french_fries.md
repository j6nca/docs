# French Fries

Serving size: 1 Potato
Prep time: 10 min
Cooking time: 30 min

## Ingredients

- Potatoes
- Olive oil
- Salt
- Any other seasonings of choice

## Kitchen Items

## Preparation

- Slice potatoes into 1 cm thick strips
- Toss with light splash of olive oil, salt and seasonings of choice
- Air fry twice at 330 F for 15 min until crispy
